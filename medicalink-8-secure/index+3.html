Астма Астма приводит к сильному затруднению дыхания с чрезмерной вязкостью слизи в бронхах и закупоркой бронхов и часто Причиной астмы является постоянное загрязнение воздуха и курение
3/ Рак легких
Это злокачественное заболевание поражает мужчин чаще, чем женщин и основной причиной является пристрастие к курению

5/ Муковисцидоз

Respiratory diseases
Flu or what is called seasonal cold
Caused by seasonal germs and the disease may last for 3 weeks and requires initial treatment with antibiotics
Asthma Asthma leads to great difficulty in breathing with excessive mucus viscosity in the bronchi and blockages in the bronchi and is often The cause of asthma is constant air pollution and smoking
3/ Lung cancer
This malignant disease affects men more than women and the main cause is addiction to smoking
4/ Bronchitis
5/ Cystic fibrosis
This disease is often hereditary and leads to difficulty in breathing due to excessive mucus and viscosity in the respiratory system and is often treated with surgical intervention
Respiratory diseases are treated by taking Necessary tests Including pulmonary tomography As well as blood gases examination to assess the level of CO2 and O2 Ventilation performance should be improved with anticoagulants and bronchodilators And oxidation performance should be improved with medications and antioxidants. Arginine and nitric oxide are considered among the most important vasodilators in the blood
